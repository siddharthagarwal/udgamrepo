Responsibilities of a Campus Ambassador

The Campus Ambassador of a college will have a few responsibilities and we expect them to fulfill the following tasks

  1. Posters will be sent through courier which are to be stuck at appropriate places.

  2. Need to mail his/her college-mates through their central mailing system regarding various events in Udgam.

  3. To post Udgam links on their departmental/college/university groups on Facebook and other social networking sites.

  4. To acquire permissions (on our behalf) for a venue to organize a Udgam event, and conduct such events under the summit's name at institutional level.

  5. To give regular updates and feedback about the awareness of Udgam in respective colleges and discuss necessary publicity strategy with our publicity Team.

Just like any member of our exclusive team here, we treat our CA's with the same respect and trust them equivalent to a core member in organizing team. As a gesture of our trust in you,with reverence, we provide you recognition certificates on behalf of Udgam, IIT Guwahati for your contribution towards the branding and publicity strategy. Also the job is not without its perks, as it includes goodies, fee waivers, incentives and much more!
